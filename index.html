User Guide for Video Gallery

Get Started

  1. Visit the Website:
    Open your browser and go to VIBEe
  2. Explore the Website:
    Once the website loads, you will see the homepage with video thumbnails.
  3. Hover Over a Video Element:
    Move your mouse over any video thumbnail. You'll see various download buttons appear.
    • Download Video Button:
      Download, Function: Downloads the video file.
    • Download All Files Button:
      Download All, Function: Downloads all associated files.
    • Download GPX Button:
      Download GPX, Function: Downloads the GPX file.
  4. Download the Files:
    Click on the desired download button. The file(s) will start downloading to your device.
